Privacy Policy

Lapsly — the no-streak days-since tracker · Last updated July 22, 2026

Lapsly is built by TigerBrands Global to be private by default. It works fully offline, needs no account, shows no ads, and keeps what you track on your own phone.

The short version

  • We do not collect, upload, sell or share your personal data.
  • Everything you enter stays in local storage on your device.
  • No accounts, no logins, no advertising, no third-party analytics or tracking.

What Lapsly stores

Lapsly stores the trackers you create (their names) and the dates you mark them done directly on your device. This information never leaves your phone through Lapsly. Uninstalling the app or clearing its data deletes it permanently.

Purchases

Lapsly offers an optional paid subscription. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play under Google's own terms and privacy policy. We never see or store your card number or billing details — we only receive confirmation from Google Play that an entitlement is active so the app can unlock its paid features.

Data sharing

We do not share your information with anyone, because we do not collect it. The only outbound connection Lapsly makes is to Google Play for purchases. No advertising, marketing or analytics services are used.

Children's privacy

Lapsly is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Security

Because your data is stored locally and not transmitted to us, it stays under your control and your device's own protections.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at this same address.
